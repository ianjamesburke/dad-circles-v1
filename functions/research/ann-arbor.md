# Ann Arbor Dad Groups & Activities

Research data for matching and context generation in the Ann Arbor/Ypsilanti area.

## Dad Meetup Groups

### The Mamas Network (Dada Division)
- **Website:** https://themamasnetwork.org/dads
- **Programs:**
  - Dadas & Littles: 1st & 3rd Saturday, 9:00 AM, Little Break Cowork
  - Dads, Decks & Donuts: 1st Saturday, 10:00 AM, Upkeep Games
- **Format:** Board games and coworking space meetups
- **Target:** All fathers with young children

### New Dads of Ann Arbor
- **Platform:** Meetup.com
- **Website:** https://www.meetup.com/new-dads-of-ann-arbor/
- **Target:** Fathers of infants and toddlers
- **Focus:** Peer support for early fatherhood challenges

### DadCircles
- **Model:** Algorithmic peer matching
- **Criteria:** Child's age + geographic proximity
- **Status:** Local pilot in Ann Arbor/Ypsilanti

### St. Nicholas Church Men's Group
- **Website:** https://www.stnickaa.org/themensgroup.html
